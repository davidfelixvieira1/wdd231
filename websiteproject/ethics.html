The ethical challenges of Artificial Intelligence highlight the need for careful consideration as we integrate this technology into society. AI systems often raise questions about transparency, accountability, and fairness. For instance, algorithms can unintentionally perpetuate biases present in the data they are trained on, leading to unfair outcomes in hiring, lending, or law enforcement. Additionally, the rise of AI-driven decision-making brings concerns about job displacement and privacy violations.
