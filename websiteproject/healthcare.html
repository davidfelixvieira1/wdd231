Artificial Intelligence in healthcare is bridging the gap between traditional medical practices and modern technological advancements. AI-powered tools are assisting doctors in diagnosing diseases more accurately and efficiently, often detecting conditions earlier than ever before. From robotic surgeries to personalized treatment plans based on genetic data, AI is transforming patient care. It also plays a crucial role in optimizing hospital operations, reducing costs, and ensuring better resource allocation.
