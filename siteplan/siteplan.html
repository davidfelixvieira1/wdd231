Site Plan: The Power of AI - An Universal Change

Site Name

The Power of AI: An Universal Change

This name was selected to emphasize how AI impacts the world universally and its transformative power in various industries.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a comprehensive resource about AI's influence on industries like healthcare, transportation, and education. It will provide articles, case studies, and interactive tools to showcase AI's benefits and ethical challenges.

Scenarios

  • How can AI improve patient care in hospitals?
  • What are the ethical challenges of AI in business decision-making?

Color Schema

The following color scheme will be used:

  • Blue (#004080): For headings and accents.
  • Light Blue (#80c1ff): For backgrounds and secondary elements.

Typography

Font Family: Roboto

  • Headings: Roboto, bold.
  • Body Text: Roboto, regular.
